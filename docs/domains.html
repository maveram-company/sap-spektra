Modo RESTRICTED:RESTRICTED mode: RESTRICTED — HARestrictedProvider (failover controlado por proveedor cloud)(failover controlled by cloud provider)

⚠️
Nota: En LOCAL_SIMULATED, la gestion de estados de HA es real (se persiste en BD), pero los comandos de failover son simulados. En AWS_REAL, el failover ejecutaria operaciones reales. Note: In LOCAL_SIMULATED, HA state management is real (persisted to DB), but failover commands are simulated. In AWS_REAL, failover would execute real operations.
