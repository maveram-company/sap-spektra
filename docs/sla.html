Definiciones de Metricas SLASLA Metric Definitions

MTTR (Mean Time To Repair)

Tiempo promedio desde que se detecta un incidente (alerta CRITICAL) hasta que se resuelve. Se calcula como (resolvedAt - createdAt) sobre todas las alertas resueltas en el periodo. Average time from incident detection (CRITICAL alert) to resolution. Calculated as (resolvedAt - createdAt) across all resolved alerts in the period.

< 15 min — Excelente< 15 min — Excellent
15-60 min — Aceptable15-60 min — Acceptable
> 60 min — Breach> 60 min — Breach

MTBF (Mean Time Between Failures)

Tiempo promedio entre fallas consecutivas de un sistema. Se calcula dividiendo el tiempo total de operacion por el numero de incidentes. Mayor MTBF indica mayor estabilidad. Average time between consecutive system failures. Calculated by dividing total operation time by the number of incidents. Higher MTBF indicates greater stability.

> 30 dias — Excelente> 30 days — Excellent
7-30 dias — Aceptable7-30 days — Acceptable
< 7 dias — Critico< 7 days — Critical

Disponibilidad %Availability %

Porcentaje de tiempo que el sistema estuvo operativo (status != critical/unreachable) durante el periodo de medicion. Se calcula a partir de los HealthSnapshots. Percentage of time the system was operational (status != critical/unreachable) during the measurement period. Calculated from HealthSnapshots.

≥ 99.9% — Excelente (Three Nines)≥ 99.9% — Excellent (Three Nines)
99.0-99.9% — Aceptable99.0-99.9% — Acceptable
< 99.0% — Breach< 99.0% — Breach

Health Score

Puntuacion compuesta 0-100 calculada a partir de CPU, memoria, disco, estado de componentes, alertas activas y breaches. Se registra periodicamente en HealthSnapshots. Composite score 0-100 calculated from CPU, memory, disk, component status, active alerts, and breaches. Recorded periodically in HealthSnapshots.

80-100 — Saludable80-100 — Healthy
50-79 — Degradado50-79 — Degraded
0-49 — Critico0-49 — Critical
